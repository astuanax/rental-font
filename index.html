Rental-icon fonts for the Rental industry

The rental-icon font contains a list of icons related to the rental industry: villas, apartments, hotels, cars, etc. We are also preparing a number of rental icons from well known rental companies such as booking.com and airbnb.

Please feel free to submit an issue with a new icon requests, or issues that you are experiencing with the font.
